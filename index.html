How the estimate works

FleekPrint uses configurable category-level demonstration factors for carbon and water savings, combined with the order's garment count and weight. All outputs are clearly labelled as estimates and can later be replaced with Fleek-approved lifecycle data.

View methodology and assumptions

Textile waste diverted: total order weight.

CO₂e avoided: order weight × category carbon factor.

Water saved: garment count × category water factor.

Impact score: a demonstration score using garment count, weight and category, capped at 99.

This hackathon prototype does not provide audited environmental claims.
